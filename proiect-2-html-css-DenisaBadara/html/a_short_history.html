    The first written set of team handball rules was published in 1906 by the Danish gym teacher, Olympic medalist Holger Nielsen .

    The official rules of handball were written down in Berlin on October 29th, 1917, by Karl Schelenz .

  • 1921

    Handball was first introduced in Romania, when the first game was played at the Central Stadium Sibiu. The game was organised by Professor Wilhelm Binder, and the teams were two local high schools: Brukenthal High School and Girls High School.

  • 1931

    In 1931, Sibiu organised the first major competition, Transylvania Cup.

  • 1936

    The Romanian Handball Federation, the governing body for handball in Romania, was established in 1936 and has since been responsible for organising and promoting the sport in the country.

  • 1962

    According to the Encyclopedia of Handball, Romania has a rich handball history and has produced many talented players over the years. The country has also hosted several major international handball tournaments, including the European Men's Handball Championship in 1962
